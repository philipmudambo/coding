Projects we are focusing on

Undugu Higher Education Fund

The project is dedicated to helping disadvantaged young individuals by offering them partial school fees scholarships and providing necessary stationery for their higher education pursuits. By doing so, we aim to ensure that these underprivileged youth have access to quality education and are not hindered by financial constraints. By providing financial support through scholarships, we also aim to create equal opportunities for all, regardless of their socio-economic background. Additionally, by supplying stationery items, they aim to remove another potential barrier to education for these students. Through this initiative, the organization strives to empower and uplift these young individuals, enabling them to pursue their dreams and achieve a brighter future.

Sista+

The project themed "Restore Her", is primarily dedicated to empowering and supporting the girl child. One of the main initiatives is providing sanitary pads to ensure that disadvantaged girls can comfortably manage their menstrual hygiene. Additionally, the project aims to rehabilitate teenage mothers by allowing them to return to school and continue their education. By offering them educational support, these young mothers can gain the knowledge and skills necessary to build a better future for themselves and their children. Furthermore, the project recognizes the importance of addressing the needs of the boychild as well. It provides membership and counseling services to boys, offering them guidance and support to navigate the challenges they may face. Overall, the project strives to create a more inclusive society that thrives.

Our Partner 

Based in Hamburg, Germany, Gesellschaft für Agrarprojekte (GFA) Consulting Group is a European consulting firm active in the development cooperation sector. It has implemented complex studies and projects worldwide since 1982 helping leading development agencies, ministries, and public clients to manage projects financed through bilateral and multilateral funds. They work in cooperation with stakeholders in government, the private sector, citizens’ groups, and Non-Governmental Organisations like for instance Undugu Empowerment Programs.

Gesellschaft für Agrarprojekte Consulting Group balances innovation and reliability to meet local needs.
